Case Study: Campfire Cooking

After over a year of restrictions on travel and exploration, due to Covid-19, many people are seeking a chance to get back out in to the great outdoors for activity and recreation. For many, nights will be spent sitting, relaxing, swapping stories round a campfire with some food and drinks. Others will be happy to stay at home and enjoy time with family, friends and neighbours sharing a bite to eat and drink around a firepit or open BBQ. The Campfire Cooking website aims to enable those individuals to find and share recipes, from around the world, that can be used to create dishes/meals over an open fire.
