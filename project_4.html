Case Study: P-L Services Website

Pathology and laboratory (P&L) services are provided in hospitals across the globe and play a vital role in the diagnosis and monitoring of disease. Each of these hospital labs spend considerable time and effort producing vast amounts of information/content for their end users; however, all to often, it doesn't reach them as they don't have the time to search and scour poorly designed websites for it. The purpose of this website is to make that information accessible to the labs end user quickly and clearly. It has been designed to set out the information logically and with clear navigation aids to ensure the user can move through the website efficiently.
